About Me

I'm Viola Sallabegolli, a 23 year old from Peja, currently living in Prishtina. I am about to start planning my bachelor's thesis in computer science. I'm a front-end developer. Besides coding i am an artist (aquoilist).

In this website you can find many works that I have done during this year, and you can be informed about the idea of aquoila and what's the meaning behind it.

"I give a special meaning to any topic, subject or theme that I have to explore. Every aquoila has special key elements that give color to the character, despite that the characters are usually in black and white." - Aquoila

Viola Sallabegolli

Work Process

Research and Plan

Research on the topic ordered by the client, planning each detail where it will be positioned and a kind of sketch of what it will look like in the end.

Design and Develop

Drawing in detailed form based on the sketch and then selecting the material to be used in each part of the drawings and finally glitters everywhere.

Deliver

The aquoilas are wrapped like gifts and the order is sent to the address requested by the client.
